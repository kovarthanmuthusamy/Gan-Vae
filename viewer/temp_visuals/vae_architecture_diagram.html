Architecture Highlights:

  • Encoder: Three independent branches with self-attention for each modality
  • Self-Attention 2D (🔍 red): Heatmap (4×4×256) and Occupancy (7×8×32) encoders
  • Self-Attention 1D (🔍 orange): Impedance (128-dim) encoder with frequency gating
  • PoE Fusion (⚙️ green): Precision-weighted combining of all three expert distributions
  • Decoder Self-Attention (🔍 red): Heatmap (16×16) and Occupancy (7×8) decoders set global structure
  • Sequential Cross-Attention (⚡ blue):
    • Step 1: Heatmap ← Impedance
    • Step 2: Heatmap ← Occupancy (initial features)
    • Step 3: Occupancy ← Impedance
    • Step 4: Occupancy ← Heatmap (updated features, not stale!)
    • ✅ Avoids circular gradient dependencies
    • ✅ Occupancy sees heatmap AFTER heatmap attended to occupancy
  • Three-Stage Decoding: Stage 1 (self-attention) → Stage 2 (sequential cross-attention) → Stage 3 (refinement)
  • All Modalities Inform Each Other: Captures full physical relationships without circular dependencies
